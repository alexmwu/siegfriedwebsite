Domer Dictionary

Academics

There are many study spots in Siegfried. The first floor has private study rooms, every section has a study room, and every floor lounge has a table for homework (pictured above).

Athletics

Siegfried is the Hall of Champions, and one of the many ways in which we display this is through interhall sports.

Sports »

Spirituality

Notre Dame is well known for its numerous masses. Siegfried comes together to celebrate Mass in the Seat of Wisdom Chapel, located on the first floor of the dorm.

Dorm Room: A Double

Siegfried offers three room types: the single, the double, and the quad, which house 1, 2, and 4 people respectively. Siegfried is also known for having some of the biggest dorm rooms on campus.

Weight Room: Get Yoked

Siegfried's weight room includes two benches, a smith machine, free weights, and much more! Outside of the weight room, we also have a treadmill for your convenience.

Laundry Room.

Our laundry room is on the first floor and has washers, dryers, and an iron and ironing board.

The Kitchen. For when you're tired of the dining hall.

Our kitchen has everything you might need, including a stove top, oven, microwave, and fridge. Just make sure to clean up after you're done!

Lounges. Take a break.

Every floor has a lounge with comfy couches, a TV, and a study space.

Game Room.

Take a break with pool, ping pong, or foosball, all of which are in the first floor lounge or outside of the weight room.
