Welcome Freshmen (and visitors)! Allow me to welcome you to our illustrious domicile. During the next few years, Siegfried will be your home. Oh, the camaraderie of it all! Anyhoo, you probably need to know a few things before moving in, right? Before we get started, please take a look at a letter from our rector, Father John Conley.


From Father John

Room Size: 12' x 14'

Mattress Size: 36" x 80" (buy long sheets)


Freshman cannot loft in the first semester, and very few loft in the second semester since the lofts in Siegfried are not modular. So what should you bring?


  • Clothing
    • Summer clothes
    • Fall/Winter clothes
    • Light jacket(s) / winter coat(s)
    • Suit/tie
    • Pajamas or ratty shorts for lounging about.
    • Underwear/socks
    • Hangers

Note: Some say its a good idea to bring a few sweaters with you but leave your heavy winter clothes at home. When you go home for fall break you can switch your summer wardrobe with wintry items. Siegfried's closets, however, are big enough to fit people in... so the choice is yours.


  • Bedding (sheets, pillow, etc)
  • Shower supplies (soap, towel, wash cloth, shampoo, flip-flops, bucket)
  • Computer (laptop recommended) / TV / speakers / console / chargers / phone / batteries
  • Toiletries
    • Toothbrush, toothpaste, dental floss, mouthwash
    • Deodorant, cologne
    • Razor, shaving cream
    • Contact solution
    • Comb, brush
  • Mini fridge
  • Plates, silverware, cups
  • Detergent, dryer sheets, hamper
  • Iron / ironing board (Also available in the laundry room)
  • A small carpet/rug for the center of the room. If you want to cover the room in carpeting, carpets cut to fit the room size can be bought from outside of the Stepan Center in the first few days.
  • Small couch / futon / recliner / bean-bag / blow-up furniture (Note that you will probably not be able to fit a small couch or futon if you don't plan on bunking)
  • Plenty of "appropriate" posters
  • Reading lamp / floor lamp
  • An air freshener
  • A basic toolkit
  • First Aid Kit, containing...
    • Band-Aids
    • Aspirin
    • Cough drops
    • Alka-Seltzer
    • Heartburn relievers
    • Nyquil
    • Thermometer
  • Anything else you and your roommate think would be cool

Keep in mind that this is meant to cover most things you might need, but it is in no way a comprehensive list. Additionally, if you wish to work on campus, make sure you bring the required documents.
