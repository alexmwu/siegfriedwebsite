Siegfried Hall prides itself on campus involvement. Here you'll find a brief list of dorm events.

Frosh-O and Move-In

Freshmen are permitted to move into their rooms the Friday before classes begin in the fall. Upperclassmen will be on campus to help freshmen get settled and to lead them through various events before classes start the next Tuesday.


Dorm Masses

The Siegfried community comes together in our very own chapel every Sunday night at 10 PM to celebrate the Eucharist. Daily Mass is also said Monday through Thursday at 10 PM.


Hall Council

Anyone interested in helping the dorm or finding out what is planned for the upcoming week needs only to show up at Hall Council Meetings every Tuesday in the Chapel Lounge starting at 10:30 PM. Free pizza will be served!


Football Pep Rallies

Before every home football game, the Notre Dame student body gathers in various locations on campus to get pumped up for the game.


Winter Formal at the Warren Clubhouse

The only dance held at the Warren, Siegfried's Winter Formal is usually held during the first weekend in December.


Texas BBQ

Hall staff cooks up sandwiches for all the students as they begin their fall exam week. This group meal is generally held the Monday night of fall exams in December.


Day of Man

Early in the spring semester, the men of Siegfried don their shorts, t-shirts, and flip flops to brave the arctic conditions of winter on campus to raise money for the South Bend Homeless Shelter. We have raised as much as $10000 in years past walking from class to class in our skimpy attires.


Spring Formal

Siegfried's second and final dance of the year takes place in early spring with tickets usually costing $10. The dance is held on campus.


Rambler Retreat

Our annual retreat usually takes place in mid-March, and took place in a state park this year, but has been across the lakes in years past.


Hall Senator Elections

The dorm elects one member to serve as Hall Senator late each February.


Hall Council Elections

The dorm elects a president and vice-president to serve the dorm late each March.


Room Picks

For returning students wishing to continue their stays in Siegfried, room picks take place in late March.


Varsity Athletics

Siegfried also sponsors a variety of varsity sports on campus. This year is men's hockey.
